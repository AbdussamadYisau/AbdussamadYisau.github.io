03 : About me

Who Am I?

My name is Abdussamad Yisau. I am a Software Engineer, who leans more towards Frontend web development . Why Frontend? I like seeing what i make come into existence. It's such a beautiful feeling seeing code that i wrote being rendered on a screen. It gives me, I think they call it endorphins.

On the days when I am not coding, i like to kickback with a novel or an article. Sometimes, i prefer to be the one putting the words to paper. Depends on my mood, really. I sort out issues relating to external engagagement for a couple of brands, so i guess i can call myself a writer.
